How to use this site

Navigate through our 4 main sections to help you find fun adventures for your littlest exporer!
  • Hikes are devided out into 4 age group sections. The results will help you find hikes that your explorer will love!
  • Junior Ranger will let you search for parks with Junior Ranger at parks near you.
  • Activites breaks down a selection of fun, kid friendly acticties and shows you results in your area.
  • Lessons will give you lesson plans for three different age group categories. Whether you are a teacher, a home-schooler or just looking for ways to get your kids interested in nature, there is something here for you!
